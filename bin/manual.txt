---Asteroids Game Manual---

- Press 'Enter' on the Title Screen to play.

- Press "Spacebar" to shoot.

- Press the left and right arrow keys to make the player ship spin in that direction.

- Press the up arrow key to make the player ship accelerate.

- Press "Enter" while playing to enter the Hyperspace (teleports randomly to another position of the map. 
Caution: you can appear and collide with an asteroid)

- Press 'P' to pause and unpause.

- Press 'Esc' to finish the game (Goes to Game Over Screen)

- Press 'Enter' on the Game Over Screen to go back to the Title Screen.
